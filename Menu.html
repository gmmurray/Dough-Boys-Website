Our Menu

Classic Glazed
This may be the most classic and typical doughnut but we guarantee ours is the best. This doughnut masters the intersection between simplicity and perfection.
Chocolate Sprinkle
What is better than a chocolate-iced doughnut you ask? We have found that answer is clearly to add sprinkles. If you do not feel convinced yet, come on in and try this doughnut.
Traditional Cake
If you want something simple, Dough Boys' Traditional Cake doughnut is perfect for you. This doughnut is less sweet but more rich, like a perfect piece of cake.
Strawberry Iced
There are two kinds of people: those who want chocolate milk, and those who want strawberry milk. We believe the same goes for doughnuts and we do not want to pick a side, so enjoy this strawberry iced doughnut if you find yourself on Team Strawberry.
Cinnamon Sugar
Cinnamon and sugar are a match made in heaven, and putting them both on a doughnut is the least we could do for the happy couple.
Powdered Cake
The sweeter version of our traditional cake doughnut, this powdered sugar covered doughnut is a great combination of rich and moist.
Glazed Old-Fashioned
Old meets new with our Glazed Old-Fashioned doughnut. This doughnut is sweet but mostly rich due to the old-fashioned style doughnut beneath the glaze.
